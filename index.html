What We Do

Coding Crew is a vibrant community of high school students passionate about programming and technology. We organize weekly coding challenges, hackathons, and workshops to help members develop their skills and prepare for careers in tech. (Top 10 AI use right here)

Our mission is to make programming accessible and exciting for everyone, regardless of their experience level. From beginners learning their first language to advanced coders tackling complex algorithms, there's a place for everyone in Coding Crew.

Code Quest Rules

General Rules

  • Team participation is encouraged
  • Some time limit
  • Python, C+++, and Java allowed
  • No external help or collaboration

Scoring System

  • Easy problems: 10 points
  • Medium problems: 20 points
  • Hard problems: 30 points
  • Bonus points for better submissions

Submission Guidelines

  • Code must compile and run
  • Inlcude all edge cases
  • Submit through the official platform
  • Late submissions don't count
